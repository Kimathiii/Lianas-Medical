About Lianas Medical Centre - Leading Hospital in Kangemi Nairobi

Lianas Medical Centre is a privately-owned low-cost community health center in Kangemi, Westlands Sub County, Nairobi County. We are the best hospital in Kangemi providing comprehensive medical services to the local community.

Started in 1998 as a small healthcare facility with less than 5 beds, Lianas has grown to a 20-bed hospital in Kangemi housed in a modern building with advanced treatment and diagnostic technologies.

We are one of the oldest and busiest medium-sized private hospitals in Westlands Nairobi, offering both maternity and clinical services. In maternity alone, we have registered over 14,000 normal births since inception. We attend to over 15,000 patients annually from Kangemi, Westlands, and surrounding areas.

As a private enterprise, we bring healthcare closer to local communities in a compassionate, dignified, and affordable way. The Social Health Authority (SHA) has contracted us to offer inpatient, outpatient, minor surgery, dental, and maternity services to all its members.

Mission

To fight poverty and suffering through health by providing affordable and accessible healthcare — essential in breaking the vicious cycle of poverty and disease in our communities.

Vision

To turn our health centre into consumer-driven healthcare with superior clinical outcomes, customer satisfaction, and a disease-free population.

Our Services

Inpatient & Daycare Services

20-bed inpatient department offering round-the-clock care for medical and maternity cases.

General Outpatient

Routine medical consultations, diagnosis, and treatment for a wide range of conditions. Daily operations staffed by qualified clinicians.

Maternity Services

Comprehensive antenatal, delivery, and postnatal care. Experienced midwives and medical staff ensure safe childbirth. Immunizations, growth monitoring, nutrition counseling, and pediatric services (KEPA & Child Welfare clinics).

Ultrasound Services

Obstetric, abdominal, pelvic, and other diagnostic imaging.

Ambulance Services

Emergency and non-emergency medical transport. Safe referrals and timely access to care.

Dental Services

General dental care: cleaning, extractions, fillings, oral hygiene education.

Pharmacy Services

Stocked with essential, high-quality medications and health supplies.

Eye Clinic Services

In partnership with DOT Glasses Kenya. Basic eye check-ups, vision screening, and prescription of corrective glasses.

Laboratory Services

Blood work, urinalysis, stool analysis, infectious disease screening.
