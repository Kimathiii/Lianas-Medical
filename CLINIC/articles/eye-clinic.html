Free Eye-Clinic Camp

Published on August 10, 2025

On Saturday, Lianas Medical Centre hosted a Free Eye-Clinic Camp that provided hundreds of residents with eye checkups, vision tests, and free prescription glasses for those in need.

Our team of ophthalmologists and optometrists worked tirelessly to ensure everyone received quality service. The event also included educational talks about eye health and prevention of common vision problems.

Highlights from the Event

  • Over 300 patients attended
  • Free prescription glasses for 120 people
  • On-site screening for diabetic retinopathy

We would like to thank our partners DOT Glasses and the Social Health Authority for their continued support in bringing healthcare closer to the community.
